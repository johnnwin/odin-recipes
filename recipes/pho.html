Recipe for Pho

Description

A warm vietnamese noodle soup that is perfect for a cold day.

Ingredients

  • Broth
  • Beef or Brisket
  • Ginger
  • Onion
  • Fish Sauce
  • Rice Noodles

Steps

  1. Add raw beef and noodles into bowl
  2. Pour the broth in so it can cook the noodles and beef
  3. Add herbs and bean sprouts
