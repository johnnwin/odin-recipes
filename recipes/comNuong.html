Recipe for Com Thit Nuong

Description

A refreshing vietnamese dish with marinated and grilled pork with vegetables and fish sauce

Ingredients

  • Broken Rice
  • Pork
  • Fish Sauce
  • Pickled Carrots and Cucumbers
  • Scallion Oil

Steps to make Cuom Thit Nuong

  1. Marinate the pork in a large bowl
  2. Prepare the carrots and cucumber
  3. Prepare the Fish Sauce
  4. Assemble the dish to be eaten
