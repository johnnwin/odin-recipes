Lasanga Recipe

Description

A delicious meatball lasagna perfectly cooked for your family.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Parmesian Cheese
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Basil Leaves
  • Lasagna Noodles

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 365 degrees
  2. Put Ingredients into large pot
  3. Assemble lasagna and put it into oven
  4. Wait until lasagna is finsished cooking in oven and serve
